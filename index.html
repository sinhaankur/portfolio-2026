 Unhosted

github.com/unhosted-ai · CLI v0.0.34

AI that lives where you do.

Pool the computers you already own — and optionally your friends', and optionally a public swarm of strangers' GPUs — into a single inference cluster. One endpoint. Mac, Linux, Windows. CUDA, Metal, ROCm.

Three trust modes. You decide the radius. Local and trusted are free forever; public is the safety net you pay for in USDC only when you opt in. You can use Unhosted for the rest of your life and never spend a dollar.

I'm a Principal UX Designer with 12+ years turning complex enterprise systems — cloud consoles, banking platforms, supply chain, B2B commerce — into experiences operators actually understand.

I work where domain-centred design meets user-centred design: pairing deeply with PMs and engineering on product strategy, owning end-to-end flows across multiple teams, and contributing to the design systems (HgDS / RDS at Oracle) that let those flows scale. Before UX, I spent a year on technical support — most tickets came down to people drowning in a complex ecosystem. That problem is still the one I solve, just at a different altitude.

Across Oracle, Deloitte Digital, Snowtint and Rage I've shipped for Banking (Retail & B2B), Salesforce, Oil & Gas, E-commerce (Web, App, Kiosk) and Supply Chain — and led design partners through client and end-user research to ship product, not pixels.

Outside of shipping product, I mentor designers across teams, sit on our User Experience Review Board reviewing other services' work, and run a design publication documenting exploratory projects to give early-career designers a place to think out loud.
